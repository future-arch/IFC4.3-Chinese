8.8.3.15 IfcExtrudedAreaSolid

注释 该实体不是标准化模式子集或实现级别的一部分。

8.8.3.15.1 语义定义（Semantic definition）

IfcExtrudedAreaSolid 通过扫掠型材定义提供的横截面来定义。挤出方向由 ExtrudedDirection 特性给出，挤出长度由 Depth 特性给出。如果平面区域有内边界（定义了孔洞），则这些孔洞将被扫掠成实体的孔洞。

生成的实体由 IfcSweptAreaSolid.Position 相对于对象坐标系定位。如果提供，它允许重新定位挤出实体。如果未提供，则默认为当前对象坐标系。ExtrudedDirection 在 IfcSweptAreaSolid.Position 定义的放置坐标系内给出。挤出方向可以是任何不垂直于放置坐标系 z 轴的方向。

示例 Figure 8.8.3.15.A illustrates geometric parameters of the extruded area solid. The extruded area solid defines the extrusion of a 2D area by an direction and depth. The result is a solid.
示例 Figure 8.8.3.15.B illustrates geometric parameters and additional positioning parameters of the extruded area solid. The extruded area solid defines the extrusion of a 2D area by an direction and depth. The 2D area, provided by a parameterized profile definition, can be positioned relative to the object coordinate system (other then by default at 0.,0. with no rotation). The result is a solid that can be repositioned within the object coordinate system.
注释 Definition according to ISO/CD 10303-42:1992 An extruded area solid is a solid defined by sweeping a bounded planar surface. The direction of translation is defined by a direction vector, and the length of the translation is defined by a distance depth. The planar area may have holes which will sweep into holes in the solid.
注释 Entity adapted from extruded_area_solid defined in ISO 10303-42.
沿革 New entity in IFC1.5

纹理使用定义

对于侧面，纹理沿侧面向上对齐，原点位于任意型材的第一个点，并沿型材外边界逆时针方向（从上方看）。对于参数化型材，原点定义在圆形轮廓（无锐边）的 +Y 延伸处，对于所有其他型材，则定义在从 +Y 延伸处开始的第一个逆时针方向的锐边处。纹理根据 RepeatS 在型材外边界的每个侧面上拉伸或重复。纹理根据 RepeatT 沿挤出轴的每个侧面上拉伸或重复。

对于顶部和底部盖板，纹理沿前后方向对齐，原点位于最小的 X 和 Y 延伸处。纹理根据 RepeatS 和 RepeatT 在顶部和底部拉伸或重复到每个面的范围内。

对于带孔洞的型材，纹理沿内侧向上对齐，原点位于任意型材的第一个点，并沿型材内边界顺时针方向（从上方看）。对于参数化型材，内侧的原点定义在圆形轮廓（无锐边，如空心椭圆或圆角矩形）的 +Y 延伸处，对于所有其他型材，则定义在从 +Y 延伸处开始的第一个顺时针方向的锐边处。

示例 Figure 8.8.3.15.C illustrates default texture mapping with a repeated texture (RepeatS=True and RepeatT=True). The image on the left shows the texture where the S axis points to the right and the T axis points up. The image on the right shows the texture applied to the geometry where the X axis points back to the right, the Y axis points back to the left, and the Z axis points up. For an IfcExtrudedAreaSolid having a profile of IfcIShapeProfileDef, the side texture coordinate origin is the first corner counter-clockwise from the +Y axis, which equals

(-0.5IfcIShapeProfileDef.OverallWidth, +0.5IfcIShapeProfileDef.OverallDepth),

while the top (end cap) texture coordinates start at

(-0.5IfcIShapeProfileDef.OverallWidth, -0.5IfcIShapeProfileDef.OverallDepth).

8.8.3.15.4 形式化命题（Formal propositions）

8.8.3.15.5 形式化表示（Formal representation） 

ENTITY IfcExtrudedAreaSolid
 SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF
	(IfcExtrudedAreaSolidTapered))
 SUBTYPE OF (IfcSweptAreaSolid);
	ExtrudedDirection : IfcDirection;
	Depth : IfcPositiveLengthMeasure;
 WHERE
	ValidExtrusionDirection : IfcDotProduct(IfcRepresentationItem() || IfcGeometricRepresentationItem() || IfcDirection([0.0,0.0,1.0]), SELF.ExtrudedDirection) <> 0.0;
END_ENTITY;

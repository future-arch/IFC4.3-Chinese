8.8.3.41 IfcSurfaceCurveSweptAreaSolid

注释 该实体不是标准化模式子集或实现级别的一部分。

8.8.3.41.1 语义定义（Semantic definition）

IfcSurfaceCurveSweptAreaSolid 是将一个区域沿位于参考曲面上的导曲线扫掠的结果。扫掠区域由 IfcProfileDef 的子类型提供。轮廓通过隐式笛卡尔变换算子在扫描的起始点放置，此时轮廓法线与该点的导曲线切线一致，轮廓的 x 轴与该点的曲面法线一致。在导曲线的任何点上，扫掠轮廓的原点位于导曲线上，轮廓的法线指向导曲线的切线方向，轮廓的 x 轴与该点处的参考曲面法线相同。

注释 The profile area''s normal has to be identical to the tangent of the directrix at any given point. In case of a directrix having a linear segment at the start point, the segment has to be perpendicular to the profile at start.

Directrix 和 ReferenceSurface 在对象坐标系中定位。扫描操作的起始点是 StartParam，参数值基于曲线参数化提供。如果未提供 StartParam，则默认值为导曲线的开头。扫描操作的结束点是 EndParam，参数值基于曲线参数化提供。如果未提供 EndParam，则默认值为导曲线的末尾。实体的几何形状不依赖于曲线参数化；体积取决于扫掠的面积和 Directrix 的长度。

注释 The StartParam and the EndParam are not normalized by default, they depend upon the parameterization of the curve. However using the IfcReparametrisedCompositeCurveSegment within an IfcCompositeCurve as the directrix allows to explicitly reparameterize the underlying sweeping curve. In case of a closed curve, such as IfcCircle or IfcEllipse, as the directrix, StartParam and the EndParam shall not exceed the parametric range, they shall not be > 360°.
示例 The reference surface is any surface (plane, cylindric, composite) situated in 3D space and positioned in the object coordinate system. In most cases, it is a plane or a surface of extrusion. The directrix lies on the surface, in case of a plane it might be defined as a polyline or composite curve, in case of a cylindrical or other non-planer reference surface it might often be defined as a p-curve on this reference surface.

在导曲线的任何点上，都可以构造一个平面。隐式平面的位置坐标系的原点位于导曲线上。位置坐标系的 Axis3（z 轴或法线）与该点的导曲线切线相同，位置坐标系的 Axis1（x 轴或 u）与参考曲面法线相同。Axis2（y 轴或 v）被构造出来。

如果省略继承的 Position 属性，则扫掠实体的所得主体不会被重新定位。否则，由 Position 属性建立的坐标系将用于相对于对象坐标系重新定位主体。

示例 图 8.8.3.41.A 说明了一个使用圆柱参考曲面和用于扫掠矩形的 p 曲线的示例。未提供 Position，因此它不会重新定位所得的扫掠实体。图 8.8.3.41.B 显示了预期的结果。
示例 Figure 8.8.3.41.A illustrates an example using a cylindrical reference surface and a p-curve for sweeping a rectangle. The Position is not provided and therefore it does not reposition the resulting swept solid. Figure 8.8.3.41.B shows the expected result.
注释 定义根据 ISO/CD 10303-42:1992 曲面曲线扫掠区域实体是一种扫掠区域实体，它是将一个面沿位于 ReferenceSurface 上的 Directrix 扫掠的结果。SweptArea 的方向与法线方向相关。
注释 Entity adapted from **surface_curve_swept_area_solid** defined in ISO 10303-42.

A surface curve swept area solid is a type of swept area solid which is the result of sweeping a face along a Directrix lying on a ReferenceSurface. The orientation of the SweptArea is related to the direction of the surface normal.

The SweptArea is required to be a curve bounded surface lying in the plane z = 0 and this is swept along the Directrix in such a way that the origin of the local coordinate system used to define the SweptArea is on the Directrix and the local x-axis is in the direction of the normal to the ReferenceSurface at the current point. The resulting solid has the property that the cross section of the surface by the normal plane to the Directrix at any point is a copy of the SweptArea.

The orientation of the SweptArea as it sweeps along the Directrix is precisely defined by a Cartesian Transformation Operator 3D with attributes: > * Local origin as point (0., 0., 0),

* Axis 3 as the direction of the tangent vector t at the point of the directrix with parameter u. The remaining attributes are defaulted to define a corresponding transformation matrix T(u), which varies with the directrix parameter u.

Informal Propositions

  1. SweptArea 应位于隐式平面 z = 0 内。
  2. Directrix 应位于 ReferenceSurface 上。

8.18.3.17.1 语义定义（Semantic definition）

IfcShapeRepresentation 代表了在特定几何表示上下文中，产品或产品组件的特定几何表示的概念。继承的属性 RepresentationType 用于定义用于形状表示的几何模型（例如 'SweptSolid' 或 'Brep'），继承的属性 RepresentationIdentifier 用于表示 IfcShapeRepresentation 所捕获的表示的类型（例如 'Axis'、'Body' 等）。

几个用于形状表示的表示标识符被包含为 RepresentationIdentifier 的预定义值。表 8.18.3.17.A 显示了 RepresentationIdentifier 的定义值列表。

IFC4.3.2.0 弃用 Representation type 'PointCloud' is now deprecated as other representation types also didn't differentiate between one or multiple geometries. Use 'Point' instead.

几个用于形状表示的表示类型被包含为 RepresentationType 的预定义值。表 8.18.3.17.B 显示了 RepresentationType 的定义值列表。

注释 The definition relates to shape_representation defined in ISO 10303-41.
沿革 New entity in IFC1.5.
IFC4 变更 The RepresentationType's 'Point', 'PointCloud', 'Curve', 'Curve3D', 'Surface', 'Surface2D', 'Surface3D', 'FillArea', 'Text', 'Tessellation', 'AdvancedBrep', 'LightSource', and the RepresentationIdentifier 'Body-FallBack', 'Profile', 'Clearance', 'Lighting' have been added.

8.18.3.17.5 形式化表示（Formal representation） 

ENTITY IfcShapeRepresentation
 SUBTYPE OF (IfcShapeModel);
 WHERE
	CorrectContext : 'IFC4X3_DEV_62887e3f.IFCGEOMETRICREPRESENTATIONCONTEXT' 
IN TYPEOF(SELF\IfcRepresentation.ContextOfItems);
	CorrectItemsForType : IfcShapeRepresentationTypes(SELF\IfcRepresentation.RepresentationType, SELF\IfcRepresentation.Items);
	HasRepresentationIdentifier : EXISTS(SELF\IfcRepresentation.RepresentationIdentifier);
	HasRepresentationType : EXISTS(SELF\IfcRepresentation.RepresentationType);
	NoTopologicalItem : SIZEOF(QUERY(temp <* Items | 
  ('IFC4X3_DEV_62887e3f.IFCTOPOLOGICALREPRESENTATIONITEM' IN TYPEOF(temp))
    AND (NOT(SIZEOF(
      ['IFC4X3_DEV_62887e3f.IFCVERTEXPOINT',
       'IFC4X3_DEV_62887e3f.IFCEDGECURVE',
       'IFC4X3_DEV_62887e3f.IFCFACESURFACE'] * TYPEOF(temp)) = 1))
)) = 0;
END_ENTITY;

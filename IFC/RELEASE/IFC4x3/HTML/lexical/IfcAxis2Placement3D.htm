8.9.3.3 IfcAxis2Placement3D

RV ⓘ
AbV ⓘ

8.9.3.3.1 语义定义（Semantic definition）

IfcAxis2Placement3D 提供在三维空间中放置项的位置和方向。属性 Axis 定义 Z 方向，RefDirection 定义 X 方向。Y 方向是派生的。

注释 The RefDirection does not have to be orthogonal to Axis.

如果省略 Axis 和 RefDirection 的属性值，则放置默认 P[1]（x 轴）为 [1.,0.,0.]，P[2]（y 轴）为 [0.,1.,0.]，P[3]（z 轴）为 [0.,0.,1.]。

Figure 8.9.3.3.A 演示了 IfcAxis2Placement3D 在三维坐标系中的定义。

注释 Definition according to ISO/CD 10303-42:1992 The location and orientation in three dimensional space of three mutually perpendicular axes. An axis2_placement_3D is defined in terms of a point (inherited from placement supertype) and two (ideally orthogonal) axes. It can be used to locate and orientate a non axi-symmetric object in space and to define a placement coordinate system. The entity includes a point which forms the origin of the placement coordinate system. Two direction vectors are required to complete the definition of the placement coordinate system. The axis is the placement Z axis direction and the ref_direction is an approximation to the placement X axis direction.
注释 Entity adapted from axis2_placement_3d defined in ISO10303-42.
沿革 New entity in IFC1.5.

8.9.3.3.5 形式化表示（Formal representation） 

ENTITY IfcAxis2Placement3D
 SUBTYPE OF (IfcPlacement);
	Axis : OPTIONAL IfcDirection;
	RefDirection : OPTIONAL IfcDirection;
 DERIVE
	 P : LIST [3:3] OF IfcDirection := IfcBuildAxes(Axis, RefDirection);
 WHERE
	AxisAndRefDirProvision : NOT ((EXISTS (Axis)) XOR (EXISTS (RefDirection)));
	AxisIs3D : (NOT (EXISTS (Axis))) OR (Axis.Dim = 3);
	AxisToRefDirPosition : (NOT (EXISTS (Axis))) OR (NOT (EXISTS (RefDirection))) OR (IfcCrossProduct(Axis,RefDirection).Magnitude > 0.0);
	LocationIs3D : SELF\IfcPlacement.Location.Dim = 3;
	LocationIsCP : 'IFC4X3_DEV_62887e3f.IFCCARTESIANPOINT' IN TYPEOF(SELF\IfcPlacement.Location);
	RefDirIs3D : (NOT (EXISTS (RefDirection))) OR (RefDirection.Dim = 3);
END_ENTITY;
